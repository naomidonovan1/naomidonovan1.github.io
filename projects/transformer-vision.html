Transformer Models of Visual Cortex

This project explores how self-attention mechanisms in transformers can model the hierarchical and predictive dynamics of the primary visual cortex (V1). Inspired by biological plausibility and recent deep learning advances, we simulate stimulus-driven activity across cortical layers.

We use synthetic drifting gratings, fit linear-nonlinear models to V1 layer-specific responses, and test whether transformers trained on this data capture canonical feedforward and feedback motifs.
